Master Baiter


By Malvern Cedras

Fish Haven is known for its amazing fishing spots. Fish is even in the name of the town! We recently inquired of one of the towns oldest, but most experienced fishermen. His name is Edgar Visser, he has been fishing for over forty years. He suggests if you really want to get a great fishing expereince, it is best to come to Fish Haven between June and September. That's when the fish are all hungry after their annual migration. All bait works during that time, from the tiniest worms to the new baiter bobbles. He says that he primarily fishes in the saratoga and hampster fishing pools, quiet and peaceful, yet fruitful! The only question needed to be asked is, "When do we go fishing, guys?"!
