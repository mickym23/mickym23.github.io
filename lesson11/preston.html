Hiking on Preston's Mountains


By Mikhail Cedras

We drive past Preston's iconic mountains each day, ever wanted to explore there? Forecast365 is here to give you a rough map about hiking the Preston Mountains. There are three different trails, each with unique fitness level requirements. Winding Snake (10mi, Moderate fitness), Daredevil (2mi, Advanced fitness), Queen's Rose (5mi, low fitness) are the three trails, but we seemed to like Winding Snake the most, challenging, yet fun. Be sure to pack sunscreen, a light lunch or snacks, and lots of water. It can get quite hot here, a baseball cap and a neck protector is ideal in summer weather. We really recommend these hiking trails. Get your friends, plan a nice hike and day out on the weekend!
