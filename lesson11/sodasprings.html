Geysers! Geysers!


By Bruno Apollo

Going to a park is nice, but going to Soda Springs National Geyser Park is great! Here you can see geysers shoot up to three meters high into the air. Perfect for all weather conditions to visit. A personalized tour guide can be requested free of charge! There are also seveal boiling mud pools. The Native Indians used these naturally occuring tools to cook their foods. Quite a fancy way to cook! Great for the family, we recommend it!

Around the national park areas, there are several world-famous restaurants. Everything is freshly prepared and delivers the best for all tourists! Have a look at Milly's Kitchen for their special trout and salmon pastries!
