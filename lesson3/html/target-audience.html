Target Audience

We hope that this site has something that everyone can take information from. The main audience that
would be using this site would be between the ages of 20 and 35. People who are single or are recently married.
Most would be on-the-go, working people who are travelling often to various places in the week, and spend time
outdoors on the weekends. These people would be using mobile most of the time for efficiency, however many also have desktops at their workplaces to view the site quickly.

Persona 1

Name: Jeannette Aquelle

Occupation: Civil Engineer

The life of Jeannette: Weekdays, she goes to her work project sites all over Preston.

She needs to know the weather each and every day to determine how much time it will take for

the buildings and bridges to be built. On weekends, she loves going out with her friends to

outdoor concerts and having picnics.

Need: Jeannette needs a weather site that can keep her up to date day to day on the current and future forecasts.

Persona 2

Name: Reuben Azrael

Occupation: Software Developer

The life of Reuben: Weekdays, he works in the office working on software.

Once a week, he is also called out to do software installations at a law firm across town.

He checks into a hotel for the evening and leaves in the morning. He needs to know what to wear during visit.

He has a wife and a one year old baby. He likes to take them outdoors on the weekends and plans a big hike every second

Saturday. He needs to know the general precipitation, forecast, and amount of pollen in the air. (He suffers from hayfever).

Need: Reuben needs a weather site that can help him determine the weather forecast and pollen count to take his family out on adventures.

Scenarios

Question: "I wonder what to wear, will it be sunny tomorrow?"

The user enters the site and looks at tomorrow's forecast for their area they entered.
The user sees that tomorrow will be sunny and decides to wear a t-shirt.

Question: "I want to go hiking in the forests by Fish Haven on Saturday, is the weather clear?"

The user enters the site and searches for the week's forecast for Fish Haven. The site shows
that Fish Haven has a high chance of precipitation on Friday and Saturday. The user sees this and
packs a raincoat and non-slipping hiking boots.

Question: "My project says that it takes 2 high-humidity days to fully set, when would that be?"

The user enters the site and searches for the humidity levels in that area. It shows that the next
high-humidity day is in three day's time. The user determines that the project will only be fully set
in five day's time then.

Question: "The news said that a hurricane would be heading for my state, will I be affected?"

The user enters the site and goes into the Storm Center tab. It shows that the hurricane would be traveling
close-by the area that he/she stays. It alerts that proper precautions should be made in case any unforeseen
problems occur.

Question: "I want to move to Soda Springs, what site would have pictures about the landscape and area?"

The user enters the site and searches for Soda Springs in the Gallery, here the user will find high quality
images of Soda Springs and the surrounding areas. The user now has the choice to pursue the move or not.
