Wedding Receptions

Here at The Temple Inn and Suites, we love to host receptions and strive to allow you to enjoy your big day.
Below is a list and description of our venues. Specific images and appointments for these receptions can be requested
through our contact form. We want the best for you and your spouse, give us a shout!


Reception Options

  • Indoor (Includes Tables and Chairs, Central Kitchen avaliable)
    • Diamond: Capacity - 120 people, air-conditioned room, wooden flooring with antique 1920's finishing. Ideal for modern weddings.
    • Ruby: Capacity - 180 people, central dancefloor, pillars for lights and visual show. Ideal for weddings with more partying.
    • Topaz: Capacity - 95 people, arty restaurant vibe, views of the vineyards out of the windows. Ideal for classic weddings.
  • Outdoor (Includes Tables and Chairs, No Kitchen Nearby)
    • Emerald: Capacity - From 100 people, great outside lawn and space for entertainment. Perfect for homemade, picnic weddings.
    • Opal: Capacity - 85 people, creative, original feeling. Wooden tables, chairs, and features. Ideal for natural, earthy weddings.
